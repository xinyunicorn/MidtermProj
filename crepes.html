This crepes recipe is simple and sweet (no pun intended) - it's basically just making really thin pancakes. The most difficult part is probably not eating them all at once (which my brother learned the hard way not to do). With these crepes, you can choose to make them sweet or savory and add whatever topping you'd like in the end :).
